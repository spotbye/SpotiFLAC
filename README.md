## FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>Is this software free?</b></summary>
<br>

Yes. No account, login, or subscription required — just an internet connection.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can this get my Spotify account banned?</b></summary>
<br>

No. This software has no connection to your Spotify account. Spotify data is obtained by reverse engineering the Spotify Web Player, not through user authentication.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Where does the audio come from?</b></summary>
<br>

Audio is fetched via third-party APIs from Tidal, Qobuz, and Amazon Music.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Why does metadata fetching sometimes fail?</b></summary>
<br>

Your IP address may have been rate-limited. Wait and try again, or use a VPN.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Why does my antivirus flag the file?</b></summary>
<br>

This is a false positive, likely caused by UPX compression. If concerned, fork the repository and build from source.

</details>
